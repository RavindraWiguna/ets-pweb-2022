Tentang Kami

Green Catering adalah layanan jasa catering dengan menu sehat sesuai dengan ketentuan diet vegetarian. Sesuai namanya, Green Catering menyediakan berbagai variasi masakan dengan bahan dasar sayur dan buah-buahan serta turunan produk hewani yang hewani yang diperbolehkan dalam diet vegetarian seperti susu dan telur.

Saat ini, Green Catering hanya beroperasi di Kota Surabaya serta menyediakan catering per jenis makanan, per hari, serta per minggu sesuai dengan keinginan Anda.

FARMERS MARKET FRIED RICE

Farmers Market Fried Rice uses all the beautiful veggies you may have in your fridge, CSA box or at your local farmer's market or local Asian Market.

TOFU STIR-FRY WITH BROCCOLINI AND MUSHROOMS

Delicious Tofu Stir-Fry with Broccolini and Mushrooms is a quick, easy weeknight dinner loaded with healthy vegetables! Vegan and GF adaptable!

BUTTERNUT RISOTTO WITH LEEKS

A quick and easy weeknight dish, Butternut Risotto with Leeks and Spinach made with very little fuss. Vegan-adaptable and Gluten-free.

INSTANT POT PINTO BEAN STEW

A simple delicious dish, Pinto Bean Stew made in an Instant pot using dry pinto beans, yams, corn, and poblanos. A hearty, flavorful and vegan-adaptable weeknight dinner.

FARMER'S MARKET VEGGIE QUESADILLAS

Vegetarian Quesadillas filled with farmer's market veggies like bell peppers, zucchini, sweet potato, greens, black beans and melty cheese (optional) seasoned with savory Mexican spices.

MEXICAN RICE WITH SHEET-PAN VEGGIES

Healthy & Flavorful Mexican Rice made with brown rice, topped with sheet-pan roasted yams, red onions, and shishito peppers. Serve with pickled jalapeños and pumpkin seeds for a comforting, delicious vegan meal.

FRENCH ONION SOUP

A cozy, comforting, classic dish, French Onion Soup with caramelized onions in rich deep broth topped with toasty croutons and melted gruyere. Vegan, Vegetarian Adaptable!

CREAMY MUSHROOM SOUP

Creamy Mushroom Soup with Garlic Chips and Rosemary – a delicious dish that perfect for special gatherings or simple enough for weeknight dinners!

MOROCCAN LENTIL AND CHICKPEA SOUP (HARIRA)

oroccan Lentil and Chickpea Soup (aka Harira) is hearty, full of protein and loaded with nutrients. Warming fragrant spices make this healthy one-pot meal deeply flavorful!
